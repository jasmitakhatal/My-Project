Description

Made with generous quantity of lime pieces, salt,
fenugreek and many other ingredient, The Lime
Pickle is an absolutely delight for the taste buds.

Ingredients

Lime pieces (60%), Salt (14%), Edible Vegetable Oil
(13%), Red chilli powder (5%), Mustard seeds,
Fenugreek, Turmeric, Asafoetida, Acidity regulator (E260), Preservative (E211)

0.07g; Saturated Fat 6.2g; Poly-Unsaturated Fat 4.5g;
Mono-Unsaturated Fat 3.7g; Trans Fat 0g;
Cholesterol 0g
